About me

I’m a junior Systems Administrator finishing a higher technical degree (ASIX). I build practical skills through personal projects, homelab environments and CTFs — focused on Linux administration, automation and applied security. I enjoy turning manual tasks into repeatable, auditable code and learning by doing.

Operations & Automation

Day-to-day I work with Linux servers, containers and configuration-as-code tools. I automate deployments and repetitive tasks using Bash, Python and Ansible; I design simple CI/CD flows and write runbooks to make operations predictable and safe.

Security & Response

I practice incident triage, log collection and threat hunting in my lab. I harden systems with secure sysctl, firewall rules and monitoring hooks; I build small DFIR helpers and learn offensive techniques through structured labs to better defend infrastructure.

Continuous Learning

I’m finishing ASIX and actively studying certifications and hands-on paths (CTFs, TryHackMe / HackTheBox). I value documentation, tests and small automations that reduce human error. I’m eager to join a team as a junior SysAdmin / SecOps to apply and grow these skills.
